STEP 4:Analise similarities between "Calculated Frequencies Table" and "English Alphabet Frequencies Table"

STEP 6:Replace characters of Cipher Text in Scratch pad with a character estimated previously using a Modify function of PART II

STEP 7:Based on Hints from Cipher text in "Scratch pad" area make more replacement of cipher text characters

STEP 8:Repeat Step 7 till you get a meaningful English Text
